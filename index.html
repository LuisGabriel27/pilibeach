Our Story

About Pili Beach

From a dream of island living to a paradise for all

Est. in Agmanic, Tablas, Romblon

A Dream That Became Paradise

Pili Beach Resort began as a personal dream of Ms. Liezl Olim-Hansen and her husband, Mr. Jan George Hansen. What started as a quiet retirement home nestled along the pristine shores of Agmanic, Sta. Fe, Tablas, Romblon, soon blossomed into something far greater than they had imagined.

Captivated by the untouched beauty of the island — its crystal-clear waters, powder-white sand, and the warm spirit of the local community — Liezl and Jan George found it impossible to keep this paradise to themselves. They envisioned a place where travelers from around the world could experience the same peace, beauty, and authentic Filipino hospitality that made them fall in love with Tablas.

With passion in their hearts and the island breeze as their guide, they transformed their retirement haven into Pili Beach Resort — A Paradise Retreat. Today, the resort stands as a testament to their love for the island, offering guests beachfront villas, poolside bungalows, thrilling water adventures, and the warmth of a family-run getaway.
